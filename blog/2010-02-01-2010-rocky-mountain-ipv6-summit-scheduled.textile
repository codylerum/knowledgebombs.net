p. The Rocky Mountain IPv6 Task Force (RMv6TF) has announced details of their 3rd annual Rocky Mountain IPv6 Summit. The summit, scheduled for May 26-27 in Denver, promises to be an excellent opportunity to learn about IPv6 technologies and trends, and also to meet other people in various stages of IPv6 deployment and/or planning.
